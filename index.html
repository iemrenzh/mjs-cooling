Knowledge Base

Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I service my AC unit?

We recommend a professional tune-up twice a year—once before the cooling season and once before the heating season—to ensure efficiency and prevent breakdowns.

What are the signs my AC needs repair?

Common signs include blowing warm air, unusual grinding or banging noises, frequent cycling, or a sudden, unexplained spike in your electricity bills.

Can a dirty filter affect my cooling?

Absolutely. A dirty filter restricts airflow, forcing your system to work much harder. This decreases comfort, increases energy costs, and can eventually lead to system failure.

Do you offer emergency support?

Yes, we provide 24/7 emergency HVAC repair services. If your system fails in the middle of the night, our technicians are just a phone call away.
